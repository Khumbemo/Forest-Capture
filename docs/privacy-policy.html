Forest Capture

Privacy Policy & Data Protection

1. Introduction

Forest Capture ("the App") is an ecological field data collection application designed for scientific research. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection regulations, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

3. Data We Collect

3.1 Data You Provide Directly:

  • Account Information: Email address and password (if you choose to create an account for cloud sync)
  • Ecological Survey Data: Species names, counts, measurements (DBH, height), quadrat/transect configurations, environmental variables, disturbance assessments, and field notes
  • Herbarium Voucher Data: Specimen taxonomy, collection metadata, and collector information
  • Germplasm Collection Data: Seed/propagule collection records
  • Photos & Media: Field photographs and voice note recordings attached to surveys

3.2 Data Collected Automatically:

  • GPS Location: Latitude, longitude, altitude, and accuracy — used for geotagging survey data and displaying your position on the map
  • Device Connectivity Status: Online/offline state — used to determine sync availability
  • Basic Crash Analytics: Anonymized error logs via Firebase Analytics — used solely to improve app stability
We do NOT collect: contacts, call logs, SMS, browsing history, advertising IDs, or any data unrelated to ecological field research.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Primary Purpose: Storing and organizing your ecological field survey data for scientific research
  • Cloud Sync: If authenticated, synchronizing your survey data across devices via Firebase Cloud Firestore
  • Analytics Computation: Calculating biodiversity indices (Shannon-Wiener, Simpson, IVI, etc.) from your survey data
  • Data Export: Generating CSV, JSON, GPX, and HTML report files from your collected data
  • App Improvement: Anonymized crash analytics to improve stability in remote field conditions

5. Data Storage & Security

5.1 Local Storage (Offline-First):

All survey data is primarily stored locally on your device using IndexedDB. The app functions fully offline — no internet connection is required for data collection, analysis, or export.

5.2 Cloud Storage (Optional):

If you create an account and sign in, your data is synchronized with Google Firebase Cloud Firestore. Firebase uses industry-standard encryption:

  • Data in transit: TLS 1.2+ encryption
  • Data at rest: AES-256 encryption
  • Authentication: Firebase Authentication with bcrypt password hashing

5.3 Photo & Media Storage:

Photos are compressed and stored locally in IndexedDB. If cloud sync is active, media may be synchronized to your authenticated Firebase account.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal data or survey data with any third parties.

Your ecological data remains entirely under your control. The only third-party service used is:

  • Google Firebase: For optional authentication and cloud sync (governed by Google's Privacy Policy)
  • OpenWeatherMap API: GPS coordinates are sent to fetch weather data (temperature, humidity). No personal identifiers are transmitted.
  • OpenStreetMap: Map tile requests may include your viewport coordinates. No personal identifiers are transmitted.

7. Your Rights (GDPR Article 12–23)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access (Art. 15): You can export all your data at any time via the Export tool (CSV, JSON, or full backup)
  • Right to Rectification (Art. 16): You can edit any survey data directly within the app
  • Right to Erasure (Art. 17): You can permanently delete your cloud account and all associated data from Settings → Account → "Delete Cloud Account & All Data". This action requires typing "DELETE" to confirm and is irreversible.
  • Right to Data Portability (Art. 20): You can export your data in standard formats (CSV, JSON, GPX) at any time
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can sign out at any time and continue using the app offline. You can also clear all local data from Settings.

8. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Retained indefinitely on your device until you explicitly delete it
  • Cloud Data: Retained in Firebase until you delete your account or request erasure
  • Account Deletion: When you delete your account, all cloud-stored data (surveys, media, settings) is permanently and irrecoverably destroyed

9. Children's Privacy

Forest Capture is a scientific research tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us for immediate deletion.

10. Permissions Requested

  • Internet: Cloud sync, weather data, map tiles
  • Fine/Coarse Location: GPS coordinates for survey geotagging
  • Camera: Field photography (quadrat, herbarium specimens, canopy)
  • Microphone: Voice note recordings
  • Storage: Exporting data files (CSV, JSON, GPX, reports)

All permissions are used exclusively for ecological data collection functionality. None are used for advertising, tracking, or profiling.
